Frequently Asked Questions

How is the data collected?

All the data on Roster comes from Onionoo. Relays and their stats are then grouped into families based on their extended_family fields.
Raw data downloaded and processed every hour using consensus data provided from Onionoo.

How are points calculated?

The points are calculated via a linear combination of the various relay stats. These include bandwidth,
consensus weight, number of relays, multiple diversity metrics of the family, etc. Each stat is assigned a coefficient/weight.
